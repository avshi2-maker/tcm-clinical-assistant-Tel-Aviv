## What to Look For:

1. **Bottom Status Bar** (bottom right corner):
   - Should show spell checker icon or status
   - Click it to enable/disable

2. **Wavy Underlines**:
   - Blue or yellow wavy lines under misspelled words
   - If you don't see them, spell checker is NOT active

3. **Quick Fix**:
   - Click on underlined word
   - Press `Ctrl + .` to see suggestions

---

## If You See NO Underlines:

The extension might be disabled. Do this:

1. Press `Ctrl + Shift + P`
2. Type: "cSpell"
3. Select: "cSpell: Enable Spell Checker"
4. Or select: "cSpell: Toggle Spell Checker"

---

## Alternative Test:

Press `Ctrl + Shift + P` and type:
**"Toggle Spell Checker"**

This will manually turn it on/off.
